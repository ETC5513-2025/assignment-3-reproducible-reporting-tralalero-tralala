Dataset Overview

The dataset contains match-level statistics for EPL football games, including scores, shots, fouls, cards, and results.

Seasons used: 2015/16 to 2024/25

Extracted variables:

  • Season

  • HomeTeam, AwayTeam

  • FullTimeResult

  • HomeShots, AwayShots

Final Dataset and Variables

Variable Descriptions of Final Dataset
Variable Description
Season The English Premier League season (e.g., '2015/16')
Team Name of the football team
TotalMatches Total number of matches played by the team that season
TotalShots Total number of shots taken by the team across the season
TotalWins Total number of wins recorded by the team that season
AvgShotsPerGame Average number of shots made by the team each game in that season
WinRatePercent Win rate percentage (TotalWins / TotalMatches * 100)

  • The final dataset contains 200 observations and 7 variables.

  • Win Rate Percent as performance indicator: Reflects a team’s ability to outperform opponents and wins yield the most league points.
